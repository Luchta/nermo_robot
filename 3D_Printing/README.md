# nermo_robot
 Hardware Subfolder
 
 All parts will have to be printed using Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) with Nylon (eos PA 2200 or similar).

## STLs to be 3D Printed

* Body
	* Body Coil (2)
	* Body Solarplexus (1)
	* Tail (1)
* Head
	* Head (1)
	* Head Camera mount (1)
	* Head Neck (1)
* Legs
	* Hip (4)
	* Leg Coil (4)
	* Leg Fore Left (1)
	* Leg Fore Right (1)
	* Leg Hind Left (1)
	* Leg Hind Right (1)
	* Leg Hind Linkage (2)
